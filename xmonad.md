- xorg-server, or xorg GROUP to be safe
- lightdm (DM) & lightdm-gtk-greeter (for login)

#### extras
- nitrogen (wallpaper browser)
- picom (compositor; animations transparent windows ..)
- xmobar (status bar)
- dmenu (searching things?)
